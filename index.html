Next-level loan approval

We bring analytics to lending and help you boost sales

Analyze hundreds of attributes

Neo provides a comprehensive scoring system that analyzes every aspect of an auto loan. Hundreds of attributes spanning customer stability, financial situation, credit bureau, loan terms, purchase vehicle, and more are analyzed in real-time to provide a score for the loan.

Increase good sales

Neo takes the guess work out of approving customers, especially those with low stability. By knowing which customers and deals are safe, lenders are able to focus on closing those deals. Using Neo, lenders typically boost their monthly sales by 15%.

Approve toughest deals

Our unique approach enables an auto lender to approve even the toughest of deals. On such deals, in addition to providing a score, Neo also specifies what downpayment, loan term, monthly payment, etc. will make the deal approvable.
