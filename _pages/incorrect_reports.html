Incorrect Reports

Neo Finance Inc. considers accuracy of consumer reports paramount to the credit reporting process. If there is incorrect information on your Neo Report, please complete the Request for Investigation of Consumer Report form so that we can research this matter further.

Our goal is to ensure that all data reported is complete and accurate. Only incorrect data may be removed from your report. Accurate data that is negative may not be removed unless required by law.

Once you've completed, printed and signed this form, please submit it via mail to Neo Finance Inc.

  • Neo Finance Inc.
  • Consumer Reporting Division
  • 700 Villa Centre Way,
  • San Jose, CA 95128

Upon receipt of the completed form, Neo Finance Inc will send your report via U.S. Postal Service to the address specified.
